## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Track your nutrition effortlessly with FoodLog**

FoodLog is a simple, privacy-focused food tracking app that uses AI to estimate calories and macros from photos of your meals. No manual entry required – just snap a photo and let AI do the work.

**Key Features:**

📸 **AI Photo Analysis**
- Snap a photo of any meal
- Get instant calorie and macronutrient estimates
- Powered by advanced AI vision technology

🍽️ **Easy Food Logging**
- Add meals in seconds with photo, barcode, or manual entry
- Track calories, protein, carbs, and fat
- Daily progress rings show how you're doing

📊 **Smart Insights**
- 7-day history with visual charts
- Weekly and monthly statistics
- See your nutrition trends over time

🔒 **Privacy First**
- Your data stays on your device
- No account required to get started
- Export your data anytime (JSON/CSV)

⭐ **Quick Features:**
- Custom calorie and macro goals
- Food presets for frequent meals
- Barcode scanner for packaged foods
- Search through your food history
- Works offline

**Perfect For:**
- Anyone tracking calories for weight management
- Athletes monitoring macros
- Health-conscious individuals
- People who want simple, no-fuss food logging

**How It Works:**
1. Open FoodLog and tap "Add Food"
2. Take a photo of your meal (or scan a barcode)
3. AI estimates calories and macros
4. Adjust if needed and save
5. Track your progress throughout the day

Download FoodLog today and start your journey to better nutrition!
