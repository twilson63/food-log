# FoodLog Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2, 2026**

## Introduction

FoodLog ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Food and Nutrition Data
- Food entries you log (description, calories, macros)
- Photos of food you choose to capture
- Timestamps of your entries

### Usage Data
- App usage patterns and preferences
- Device information (type, OS version)
- App settings and configuration

### AI Processing
- When you use the photo analysis feature, images are sent to our AI service (OpenAI) for nutrition estimation
- This processing is temporary and not stored by the AI provider

## How We Use Your Information

- **Track your nutrition**: Store and display your food entries
- **AI Analysis**: Process food photos to estimate nutrition
- **Improve our service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance the app
- **Provide support**: Respond to your inquiries and feedback

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Data may be synced to our servers for backup and cross-device access
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your food logs with third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes

We MAY share data:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal requirements
- To protect our rights or safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data we hold about you
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your data
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Disable data collection features

## Children's Privacy

FoodLog is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication
- Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Third-Party Services

- **OpenAI Vision API**: Used for food photo analysis
- **Expo/React Native**: App platform
- See OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/privacy

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of FoodLog constitutes acceptance of changes.

## California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## GDPR Compliance

For EU users, we comply with GDPR requirements:
- Lawful basis: Legitimate interest and consent
- Data minimization: Only collect what's needed
- Purpose limitation: Use data only for stated purposes
- Retention: Delete data when no longer needed
