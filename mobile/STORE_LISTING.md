### Title
FoodLog - AI Calorie Tracker

### Subtitle (30 char max)
AI-Powered Food Tracker

### Keywords
food tracker,calories,nutrition,diet,macros,calorie counter,food diary,weight loss,meal tracker,eating

### Description
Track your meals effortlessly with AI-powered nutrition analysis!

**HOW IT WORKS:**
Simply snap a photo of your food, and FoodLog's AI instantly estimates calories, protein, carbs, and fat. No more tedious manual entry – just point, shoot, and log.

**KEY FEATURES:**
• 📸 AI Photo Analysis - Take a photo and get instant nutrition estimates
• 📊 Daily Tracking - Monitor your calories and macros throughout the day
• 📈 History & Stats - View your nutrition history and trends
• ⚡ Quick Manual Entry - Add foods manually when you prefer
• 🎯 Custom Goals - Set your own calorie and macro targets

**PERFECT FOR:**
- Anyone tracking calories for weight management
- Fitness enthusiasts monitoring macros
- People who want easy food logging
- Health-conscious individuals

FoodLog uses advanced AI (GPT-4 Vision) to analyze your food photos, providing accurate calorie and macro estimates in seconds. No more searching databases or guessing portion sizes – let AI do the work!

Your data stays private. Photos are processed temporarily for analysis and never stored by our AI provider.

Download FoodLog today and make nutrition tracking simple!
